Look at the chart above. These are the numbers we will be learning with today. As you can see, each number has a set of star above them. When adding numbers together, we must count all the numbers together. That is what this level will teach. By pressing the Add buttons, you will see stars appear. Look at the numbers that come up, and try to put the same amount of stars on each side of the plus sign. Then you can count all the stars that you see and put that answer in the box. If you make a mistake, just press the Reset Stars button. Good Luck, and have fun.
